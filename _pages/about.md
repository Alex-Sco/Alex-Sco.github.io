I am a Ph.D candidate at the School of Astronomy and Space Science, Nanjing University. My expected graduation time is June 2027. I am currently a visiting Ph.D student at Leiden Observatory until October 2026. 

My current researches focus on the measurements of the CNO isotopic ratios in the Milky Way, especially in the Galactic outer disk. The second topic is the Oxygen-bearing chemistry in PDR regions. Additionally, I am also interested in excitation mechanisms and non-LTE radiative transfer of molecular line transitions in clouds and star-forming regions. Some of these topics are on-going projects. More details are in publication page and project page.

Besides Astronomy, I am also interested in Geography, Biology and Psychology. I also love mobile phone photography, scientific fictions (favorate books: <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hyperion_Cantos">The Hyperion Cantos</a>, <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Summa_Technologiae">Summa Technologiae</a>, and <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Remembrance_of_Earth%27s_Past">Remembrance of Earth's Past</a> ) and rock musics (favorate band: <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dire_Straits">Dire Straits</a>).
